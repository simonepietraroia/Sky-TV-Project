Our Mission

At Sky Engineering, we are committed to continuous improvement and fostering ongoing growth within our engineering teams. The Sky Health Check System enables teams to assess their strengths and identify areas for growth and improvement by using Spotify's squard Health methodology.

What is the Health Check System?

The Health Check System provides a structured approach for engineering teams to evaluate key aspects of their workflow, such as code base quality, testing, collaboration and team morale. Using a traffice light voting system - Green indicating strong performance, Amber for areas needing improvement, and Red highlighting significant concerns. This clear and visual representation helps leaders gain valuable insights, make well-informed decisions and faster continuous improvements.

Our commitment to You

Our goal is provide valuable insights to drive better decison-making and team growth by simplifying team health assessements, Your feedback plays a crucial role in making this platform successful.
