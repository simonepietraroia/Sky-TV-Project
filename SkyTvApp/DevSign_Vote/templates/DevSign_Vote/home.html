About this Platform

DevSign_Vote is an intuitive platform designed to support engineering teams evaluate their overall performance.

Our Mission

At Sky Engineering, we are committed to continuous improvement and fostering ongoing growth within our engineering teams. The Sky Health Check System enables teams to assess their strengths and identify areas for growth and improvement by using Spotify's squad Health methodology.

What is the Health Check System?

The Health Check System provides a structured approach for engineering teams to evaluate key aspects of their workflow.

This clear and visual representation helps leaders gain valuable insights, make well-informed decisions and faster continuous improvements.

  • Green - Everything is functioning effectively
  • Amber - There are some minor concerns that may require improvement.
  • Red - There are serious issues that require urgent attention.

These votes provide us with a clear snapshot of how the team feels about vital topics such as code base quality, testing, collaboration and team morale.

Our commitment to You

Our goal is provide valuable insights to drive better decision-making and team growth by simplifying team health assessements, Your feedback plays a crucial role in making this platform successful.
