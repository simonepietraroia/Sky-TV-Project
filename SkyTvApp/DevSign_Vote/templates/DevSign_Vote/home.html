About this Platform

DevSign_Vote is an intuitive platform designed to support engineering teams in evaluating their overall performance.

Our Mission

At Sky Engineering, we are committed to continuous improvement and fostering growth within our engineering teams. The Sky Health Check System enables teams to assess their strengths and identify areas for growth using Spotify's Squad Health methodology.

What is the Health Check System?

The Health Check System provides a structured approach for teams to evaluate key aspects of their workflow.

This visual summary helps leaders gain insights, make informed decisions, and drive continuous improvement.

  • Green – Everything is functioning effectively.
  • Amber – Some minor concerns that may need improvement.
  • Red – Serious issues that require urgent attention.

These votes provide a clear snapshot of how the team feels about topics like code quality, testing, collaboration, and morale.

Our Commitment to You

Our goal is to simplify team health assessments and deliver insights that drive better decisions and team growth. Your feedback is crucial to the success of this platform.
